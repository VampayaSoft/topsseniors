Services

  • Exercise classes to help improve mobility, endurance and function, through activities that promote improved posture, balance and motion.
  • Conditioning exercises and activities to improve alertness and daily function.
  • Breathing exercises and activities to promote increased oxygen to the body and brain, improving alertness, endurance, sleep, mental and physical function.

Staff

Nichole "Nikki" Blake-Little

The founder of T.O.P.S. is Nichole "Nikki" Blake-Little. Nikki has a degree of Bachelor of Science in Nursing. She has over 16 years of nursing and senior care experience, ranging from in-hospital acute care to serving as a Director of Nursing in a senior living community. Nikki is certified in Dementia/Alzheimer's care. She also volunteers on the board of directors of Elder Options for Lake County, Florida.

Nikki is passionate about enhancing the safety and quality of life of seniors. She believes that seniors are owed honor - as many of their sacrifices have paved the way to a better life for her generation. Nikki was raised by her adorn great-grandmother Mable Montgomery. Therefore, she has a particular appreciation for the strength, resilience and wisdom that the present generation of seniors brings. There is no one more trusted to advocate for your family and the elders in your life.

Marie "Angie" Prosper

Angie is certified both as a Nursing Assistant and as Chemical Dependency Technician. Her decades of experience with both pediatric and geriatrics patients have prepared her to oversee the operations at T.O.P.S. Angie cares for others as her own family, giving them the care, love, and attention they need and deserve.
